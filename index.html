Why Choose Us

Forward-Thinking Healthcare Education Consultancy

Catalina Health Consulting is a forward-thinking healthcare education consultancy dedicated to optimizing health literacy for students, international educated nurses, agencies and diverse health care organizations.

Empowering Healthcare Professionals Worldwide

Our philosophy, deeply rooted in transformative learning, mentorship, resilience, and critical thinking, drives our mission to empower healthcare professionals worldwide. Through strategic partnerships, we aim to create a lasting impact on emergency department nursing and nursing education globally. Additionally, we are dedicated to supporting Internationally Educated Nurses (IENs) in gaining a comprehensive understanding of the Canadian healthcare system.

Supporting Internationally Educated Nurses (IENs)

Our commitment to global impact, virtual training, and collaboration with experienced educators sets us apart. We offer a holistic education model that reaches a diverse, global audience while specifically addressing the unique needs of Internationally Educated Nurses (IENs) transitioning into the Canadian healthcare system.

Our Services

1: 1 Consultation-

Providing personalized 1:1 consulting to individuals, both in Canada and internationally, offering insights and information about the British Columbia healthcare system. This encompasses assistance in navigating the intricacies of the healthcare system and showcasing available services and resources for students, international nurses, professionals, and organizations.

Group seminars, Virtual teaching

Offering group seminars and virtual teaching options customized to the specific needs of clients. Whether it's navigating healthcare complexities or providing tailored educational sessions, our services cater to diverse requirements.

NCLEX Exam support

Supporting students on their journey to NCLEX success through specialized review sessions. Our goal is to ensure a smooth transition into the professional realm.

Curriculum Design

Designing and developing healthcare-related curricula for organizations and institutions. Our expertise lies in tailoring educational content to meet the unique needs of healthcare entities.

Our Story

In the heart of Catalina Health Consulting lies a profound story, one rooted in the personal journey of its founder. Originating from East Africa, she traversed the complicated path of immigration to Canada, acutely aware of the healthcare challenges her family faced back home. Armed with this knowledge and driven by an intense desire to make a difference, she set out on a mission.

Having delved deep into the difficulties of Western health care through emergency nursing, teaching, and clinical consulting, the founder recognized a critical need. Catalina Health Consulting emerged as a response to a global call – a call for nurses worldwide to possess not just medical knowledge, but the confidence, autonomy, and critical thinking skills to stand on equal ground with physicians.

This story isn't just about healthcare consultancy; it's about breaking barriers. Catalina Health Consulting envisions a world where nurses collaborate with doctors, not merely follow orders. Beyond Western medicine, the organization champions the idea that healthcare is diverse, with no one-size-fits-all approach. The organization's purpose extends beyond borders. It's about creating networks, building relationships, and sharing knowledge globally.

Catalina Health Consulting is an inspiration of empathy and insight, thanks to its founder's personal journey. Her unique blend of academic prowess – a Masters in Health Leadership and Policy, a Bachelor's in Nursing, and an Arts degree – coupled with frontline experience, sets Catalina apart. This consultancy isn't just an education service; it's a guiding force, ensuring that the narrative of healthcare education aligns with the diverse needs of students, nurses, and organizations worldwide.

Catalina Health Consulting, at its core, is a story of empowerment, bridging gaps, and sharing knowledge to transform global healthcare. Choosing Catalina Health Consulting means entering a supportive and mentorship-driven relationship. We prioritize the relational aspect of education, offering more than just services. Trust us for personalized support and guidance as you navigate the complexities of healthcare education. Your success is our commitment, and we're here to empower you every step of the way. Choose Catalina for a transformative and supportive journey in your healthcare education.
